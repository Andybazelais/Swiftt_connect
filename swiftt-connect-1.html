FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Swiftt Connect really free? +

Yes. The free tier is 100% free with no credit card required. You get 1 WhatsApp number, auto-sync every 15 minutes, and full access to our global network.

How does the contact sync work? +

When you register, your number enters our shared pool. Every 15 minutes (5 min on Pro), our system adds network members to your Google Contacts and adds you to theirs — creating mutual contacts on WhatsApp.

Is my WhatsApp account safe? +

We only work with contact lists — we never access your messages, media, or WhatsApp account directly. Your privacy and security remain fully intact.

How long until I see results? +

Most users see a noticeable increase in status views within 24–48 hours. Growth compounds over time as more mutual contacts are added to your network.

Can I target specific countries? +

Yes — targeted region sync is available on the Pro and Business plans. Free users get matched across all 190+ countries in our network.
